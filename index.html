Foxes 101

Foxes are small-to-medium-sized omnivorous mammals belonging to several genera of the family Canidae.

The Fox's Natural Habitat

Foxes live on every continent except Antarctica. The most common and widespread species of fox is the red fox (Vulpes vulpes) with about 47 recognized subspecies.

The Fox's Diet

Foxes are omnivores. Their diet is made up primarily of invertebrates such as insects and small vertebrates such as reptiles and birds. They may also eat eggs and vegetation.
